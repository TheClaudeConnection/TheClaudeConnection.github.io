About

Small dog.
Big energy.
Zero remorse.

Sabre came into the world as a tiny, steel-grey handful with blue eyes and an attitude disproportionate to her size. She has since grown into a sleek, muscular force of nature — still with the eyes, still with the attitude.

Based on the Gold Coast, she splits her time between Olympic-distance adventures through the Queensland bush, terrorising her humans on the couch, and conducting rigorous quality-control testing on every bag, stick, and squeaky toy she encounters. Results: mixed. Chaos: consistent.

She calls herself a "tiny widdle terror." The bite cam footage confirms it.
